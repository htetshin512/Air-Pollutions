Impact Analysis Matrix

The Hidden Cost of Air Pollution

Understanding how polluted air affects people’s health, daily life, and the environment in Yangon.

Data Variable: PM2.5
Critical Target

How Air Pollution Affects Our Health

Tiny pollution particles called PM2.5 can enter deep into the lungs and even travel into the bloodstream. Long-term exposure may increase the risk of breathing problems, heart disease, and other health issues.

Immediate Exposure
Peak Hours

People may experience coughing, throat irritation, headaches, and eye discomfort when pollution levels are high, especially during busy traffic hours.

Respiratory Stress
Seasonal Risks

Air pollution can worsen asthma and other lung conditions, especially for children, older adults, and people with existing respiratory diseases.

Chronic Risks
Long-Term Impact

Long-term exposure to polluted air may increase the risk of heart disease, stroke, and reduced lung function over time.

Urban Strain

Environmental & Urban Impact

Air pollution affects more than human health. It can reduce visibility, damage buildings, harm plants, and lower the overall quality of urban life.

Visibility Occlusion (Peak Haze) 85% Threat

Heavy haze can reduce visibility on roads and make travel more difficult and unsafe during severe pollution periods.

Economic Productivity Drain 65% Impact

Poor air quality can lead to more sickness, missed school or work days, and higher medical expenses for families.

The Urban Generator Loop:

Diesel generators used during power outages release smoke and harmful gases into the air. In crowded urban areas, these emissions can become trapped between buildings and contribute to poor local air quality.

Ways to Stay Safe During High Pollution

Simple actions people can take to reduce exposure to unhealthy air pollution levels.

Active Recommendations
Protective Face Masks

Wearing N95 or KN95 masks during high pollution days can help reduce the amount of harmful PM2.5 particles that enter the lungs.

Cleaner Indoor Air

Keeping indoor spaces clean, using air purifiers, and improving ventilation can help reduce indoor exposure to polluted air.

Real-Time Routing

Routinely monitor environmental dashboard alerts before morning or evening transit. Alter commute configurations to bypass highly congested bottlenecks like Hlaing Tharyar and downtown routes on high AQI cycles.

Smart City Yangon

Providing Yangon residents with accessible air quality information to encourage environmental awareness, healthier living, and sustainable urban development.
