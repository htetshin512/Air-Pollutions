Who We Are

About Our Project

Moving toward a cleaner, healthier, and more conscious future for Yangon.

We are a student-led team developing a Smart City Yangon platform that visualizes air quality and pollution data in a clear and accessible way. The project focuses on transforming complex environmental information into simple, easy-to-understand visuals for the public.

The platform is based on internationally recognized air quality concepts and environmental health guidelines. It highlights how pollution levels vary across different areas of Yangon and explains the main contributing factors, including traffic emissions, industrial activity, and waste burning, which are common in rapidly growing urban cities.

Our Vision

To raise awareness about air pollution by making environmental data simple, visual, and accessible so people can better understand the quality of the air they breathe.

Our Mission

To develop a smart city system that presents air quality information based on collected data principles and environmental research. The system aims to help users understand pollution levels, identify key sources, and explore how urban activities influence air quality patterns in Yangon over time.
