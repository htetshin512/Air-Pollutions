Yangon Air Quality Map

Visual air pollution level map presenting pollution zones and urban air quality conditions across Yangon's major townships.

Map Key & Information
Good Air Quality

Fresh and clean air with minimal pollution. Perfectly safe for outdoor exercises and healthy breathing conditions.

Moderate Air Quality

Air quality is acceptable, though ultra-sensitive individuals may occasionally experience mild respiratory discomfort.

Unhealthy Air Quality

High pollution concentrations. May affect breathing, especially among children, outdoor workers, and elderly groups.

Rivers & Waterways

Major river systems and natural water currents flanking the main metropolitan parameters.
